### ‚ùì Troubleshooting

**Not seeing the popup?**
- Make sure the extension is enabled
- Try double-clicking to select the number
- Reload the webpage

**Number not recognized?**
- Only pure numbers work (no "$" sign)
- Decimals and commas are OK
- Examples that work: 99.99, 1,500, 42

**Settings not saving?**
- Click "Save Settings" button
- Make sure your number is valid
